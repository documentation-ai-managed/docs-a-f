## Overview

A F lets you group documents in flexible spaces called "Projects."

## Steps

1. Click "New Project" on the dashboard.
2. Name your project and add members.
3. Use folders to organize topics or sections.
4. Drag and drop files to reorder.
5. Share project links for easy access.

<Callout kind="info">
You can have unlimited projects and folders in A F.
</Callout>

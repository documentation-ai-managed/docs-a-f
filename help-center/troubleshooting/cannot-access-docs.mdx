## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" message
- Docs not visible on dashboard

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Confirm you are added as a member in the project settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and relogin">
    Log out, refresh, and sign in again at `https://af-docs.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Owner">
    Ask the project owner to update your access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
